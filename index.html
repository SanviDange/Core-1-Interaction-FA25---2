Shuffled: A Card Collection

A collection of easy or difficult card games for your next game night.

General Rules

A deck of cards typically has 52 cards. There are 4 suites-Hearts, Diamonds, Spades, Clubs (the 4 shapes). Each suit contains 13 cards ranked from lowest to highest: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack (J), Queen (Q), King (K), and Ace (A). In some games, Aces can be low or high. Standard decks include 2 jokers, which are used in some games as wild cards but removed in others.
