Common Terms

Hit

When it’s your turn, ‘hitting’ means requesting for another card.

Bust

When the sum of your cards exceeds 21, you ‘bust’ i,e. you lose the round.

Stand

When you ‘stand’ or keep the hand you are dealt with and choose not to ‘hit’.

Push

When both, you and the dealer end the rounds with the same hand value, it's a tie. Your bet is ‘pushed’ or returned.

Split

If you’re given two of the same cards as your starting hand, you can separate the pair and play two hands. You’ll need to double your wager.
